About us

About Us

Welcome to Remine India , a recognized startup committed to sustainability through responsible recycling practices. We specialize in recycling electronic waste (e-Waste) and Lithium (Li) Batteries, with a mission to contribute to a greener future by reducing the environmental impact of these materials. Our team is dedicated to upholding the highest standards of recycling, ensuring that hazardous substances are properly handled and disposed of, and valuable resources are recovered for reuse. With our expertise and cutting-edge recycling technologies, we strive to make a positive impact on the environment and create a more sustainable world for generations to come. Remine India handles every e-waste products in a safe, acquiescent and green manner. We vision to ensure maintaining proper e-waste management of your business surroundings to make it vibrant and productive.

Why Choose Us

Environmentally Responsible

At Remine India, we are committed to environmentally responsible recycling practices. Our processes comply with all local and national regulations, ensuring that e-Waste and Li Batteries are recycled in an environmentally friendly and sustainable manner.

Innovation and Technology

We utilize innovative and advanced technologies to recycle e-Waste and Li Batteries efficiently and effectively, minimizing waste and maximizing resource recovery.

Positive Impact

By choosing Remine India, you are contributing to a positive impact on the environment and society by reducing the harmful effects of e-Waste and Li Batteries on our planet.

Our Services

e-Waste Recycling

We specialize in recycling various types of e-Waste, including electronic devices, appliances, batteries, and more. Our recycling process ensures proper handling, dismantling, and recycling of e-Waste components to minimize environmental impact.

Li Battery Recycling

We have expertise in the recycling of Lithium (Li) Batteries, which are commonly used in portable electronics, electric vehicles, and renewable energy systems. Our Li Battery recycling process focuses on resource recovery and proper disposal of hazardous materials.
